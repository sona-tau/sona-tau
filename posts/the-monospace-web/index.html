    The Monospace Web

    Aesthetic created by Oskar Wickström

    I know lots of places on this website say “This is my website”, “Hello there, this is my stuff”, and other messages that might imply that all of the things presented here are my personal work. Hopefully I’ve been clear enough in giving credit where credit is due. In this case I actually want to give a shoutout to Oskar Wickström the original creator of 95% of this template. You can see their original site at The Monospace Web.

    I have tweaked the following:

    • I use IBM Plex Mono, following in a similar aesthetic as mebrane.io
    • I have several shortcodes for rendering toki pona (not really a design thing but it’s different than Oskar’s original implementation)
    • Dark grainy background instead of pure black.
    • Orange highlights very similar to the work in sacred.computer

    All in all, these are very small changes to the original work by Oskar and as such I cannot say that this design is my own.
