Here are several excerpts from miscellaneous texts that I have translated into toki pona .

ijo li ike tawa kulupu pi pipi waso la ona li ike tawa pipi waso

That which is not good for the beehive, cannot be good for the bees. - Marcus Aurelius

mi kulupu e jan pali en jan jo ala pi jo mute en jan pi pilin ike en jan pi kalama ike en jan pi wile ike en jan ike. ijo ni li ijo tan sona ala e pona anu ike. taso mi lukin e pona. pona li pona. mi lukin e ike. ike li ike. mi lukin e jan pi pali ike. jan pi pali ike li sama mi. nasin jan pi pali ike li ijo e sewi li sama nasin mi li ijo e sewi. jan pi pali ike li pakala ala e mi. jan ala li pona e mi ike. mi ijo ala e mi. mi ike ala e mi. pona li pali e mi. mi pona e mi ili sama noka li sama luka li sama oko selo li sama uta walo kiwen. mi utala e taso. taso li lawa ike e mi tan li jan.

I shall meet with the busy-body, the ungrateful, arrogant, deceitful, envious, unsocial. All these things happen to them by reason of their ignorance of what is good and evil. But I who have seen the nature of the good, that it is beautiful, and of the bad, that it is ugly. And the nature of them who does wrong, that it is akin to me, not only of the same blood or seed, but that it participates in the same portion of the divitiy. I can neither be injured by any of them, for no one can fix on me what is ugly, nor can I be angry with my kinsperson, nor hate them. For we are made for co-operation, like feel, like hands, like eyelids, like the rows of the upper and lower teeth. To act against one another is to be vexed and to turn away.

nasin weka li pali e nasin suli.

“Short cuts make long delays,” argued Pippin. - J.R.R. Tolkien, The Fellowship of the Ring
