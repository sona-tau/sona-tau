    toki pi kulupu pini

    Sona Tau Estrada Rivera
    Published on January 20, 2025, modified on December 7, 2025.

    The Cult of Done is a manifesto written by Bre Pettis with Kio Stark in August of 2016.

    • English manifesto

    Un momento,

    The header of the Cult of Done manifesto is written in “first-person English” which means that all references to “me” will be “mi” in toki pona. However, I (jan Sona) am not the author of this, I am a translator.

    toki a la jan pi (kulupu pini) . zz

    mi li pana e toki pi (pali pini) tawa sina. zz
    ni li sitelen tan mi en jan [kasi ilo open] [sike utala tan akesi kepeken e] zz
    zz zz zz lon mute1tenpo lili tan mi jo e mute1tenpo lili. zz

    toki pi (kulupu pini) li ni zz


    wan. zz
    zz sina lon e lon tu wan la zz
    zz zz zz zz lon tu wan li lon e ni3sona+ala en pali en pini. zz

    tu. zz
    zz o sina jo e ni3ali li pini ala. ni li pona e pali pini. zz

    tu-wan. zz
    zz lon pali ante li lon-ala. zz

    tu-tu. zz
    zz sina sona+lili sama lili sina sona+suli la zz
    o sina jo e ni3sina jo e sona+suli taso sina sona+lili. zz

    luka. zz
    zz o sina awen ala. zz
    zzzz sina pali pini e ijo lon luka tu tenpo suno la
    zz o sina lawa+ala e ijo. zz

    luka-wan. zz
    zz sina pali pini e ijo tan sina pali pini ala e ijo ni zz
    zzzzzzzzzz taso tan sina pali pini e ijo ante. zz

    luka-tu. zz
    zz tenpo pi (pali pini) la o sina lawa+ala e ijo. zz

    luka-tu wan. zz
    zz o sina toki e aaa tawa ijo+pona. zz
    zz zz ni2li awen e sina kama pali pini. zz

    luka-tu tu. zz
    zzzzzzzzzzzzzz jan pi (ko+ala lon luka) li ike. zz
    zz sina pali e ijo la sina jo e ko lon luka. zz
    zzzzzzzzzzzz sina jo e ko lon luka la sina pona. zz

    luka-luka. zz
    zz pali pini ike sama pali pini. zz
    zz o sina pali pini ike. zz

    luka-luka wan. zz
    zz pakala sama lili pali pini. zz

    luka-luka tu. zz
    zz sina jo e ijo pali taso sina pana e ijo pali lon linluwi la
    zz ni2sama pali pini lili. zz

    luka-luka tu-wan. zz
    zz pali pini li pana e mute. zz
