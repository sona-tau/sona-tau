Trivial toki pona Substitutions

toki open There is a family of programming languages that are called “Trivial Brainf*ck Substitutions” (TBFS for short). They basically take the 8 characters used in Brainf*ck and represent them using other strings of characters. This is (somewhat) the same idea behind sitelen pona and sitelen kuwi. I would even guess that it’s the same idea behind sitelen sitelen, but I don’t know enough yet about sitelen sitelen to make that conclusion.
